Previous Year Success Story
of Ezekiel


In the previous year, Ezekiel initiated a project that aimed to inspire civic engagement and global awareness among his students.

He organized a model United Nations (UN) conference within the school, where students represented different countries and debated real-world global issues.

Ezekiel guided his students through extensive research on their assigned countries and the topics to be discussed.

He encouraged critical thinking, negotiation, and diplomacy as they engaged in passionate debates during the conference.
